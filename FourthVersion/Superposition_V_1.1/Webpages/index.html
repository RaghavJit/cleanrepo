TO VERIFY SUPERPOSITION PRINCIPLE

Instructions

  • Choose values of R1, R2, R3 and Power-Supplies by adjusting the sliders.
  • Make connections and connect both Power Supplies to curcit.
  • Click 'CHECK' button to check connections.
    • Connect ammeters (in series) to resistances, any ammeter can be connected to any resistor.
    • Connect positive and negative of both power supplies to the curcit.
  • Turn on the MCB and Power-Supplies.
  • Click the 'ADD' button to add observations to the table.
  • Connect only one of the Power Supply, and check your connections.
    • Connect voltage source.
    • Leave connections for current supply open to as current supplies have infinite internal resistance.
  • Click on the 'ADD' button to add the values to observation table.
  • Repeate the above steps, but connecting the other Power Supply.
    • Connect current souce/
    • Short curcit the connections for voltage source as voltage supplies have zero internal resistance.
  • Click 'CALCULATE' to varify that the readings match.
