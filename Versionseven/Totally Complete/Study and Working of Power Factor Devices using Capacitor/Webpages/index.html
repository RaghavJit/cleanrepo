Study and Working of Power Factor of Devices using Capacitor

Instructions

  • Choose a load by clicking on the 'LOAD' button.
  • Make connections by dragging the nodes.
      • 1->3, 2->4, 5->11, 6->12, 5->7, 8->9, 10->13, 14->17, 14->18, 15->19, 16->20 (for tubelight: 19->21, 22->23, 24->20)
    • Connect input of variac to MCB. (blue nodes should be connected to blue and red nodes to red)
    • Connect output of variac to the voltmeter.
    • Connect fuse and switch to each other, in series.
    • Then connect positive (output) of variac to fuse-switch arrangement and extend its connection to positive of voltmeter.
    • Connect negative of ammeter, to M and C of the wattmeter.
    • Connect V of wattmeter to positive of load, and L to negative of load.
  • Check your connections by clicking on the 'CHECK' button.
  • Turn on the MCB and adjust the value of output voltage on variac, until the voltage is 230V.
  • Click the 'ADD' button to add the values to the observation table.
  • Click the 'CALCULATE' button to calculate the powerfactor of the load.
  • Change the load and repeat the above process, to get powerfactor of all the other loads
