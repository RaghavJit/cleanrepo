TO VERIFY MAXIMUM POWER DISTRIBUTION THEOREM

Instructions

  • Choose values of Rl, R1 and PowerSupply.
  • Set up the circuit for experiment.
    • Connect Multimeter across the Load resistance Rl.
    • Connect ammeter in series with R1, conncet voltmeter across Rl.
    • Connect PowerSupply to the circuit.
    • Click on 'CHECK' button to check your connections.
  • Click on 'ADD' to add observations to table.
  • Change the value of Rl and click 'ADD' to add observations to table.
  • Keep changing values of resistance until the Power Transfered is Maximum.
