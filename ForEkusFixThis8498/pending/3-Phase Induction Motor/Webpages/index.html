TORQUE SPEED CHARACTERISTICS OF 3-ϕ INDUCTION MOTOR

Instructions

  • Make connections for all the meters.
    • Connect the red node (MCB) to M and C of wattmeter-2.
    • Connect yellow node to positive of voltmeter, V of wattmeter-2 and V of wattmeter-1.
    • Connect to negative of voltmeter and positive of ammeter.
    • Connect negative of ammeter to M and C of wattmeter-1.
    • Connections for yellow, red and blue can be exchanged, also wattmeter-1 and wattmeter-2 can be exchanged.
  • Connect the circuit to loads.
    • Connect L of wattmeter-1 to A0.
    • Connect L of wattmeter-2 to B0.
    • Connect V of wattmeter-2 to C0.
  • Make connections of loads in STAR configuration.
    • Connect A1 to B1.
    • Connect B1 to C1.
  • Switch on the MCB and click on 'ADD' to add observations to the table.
  • Click on reset button to reset the connections for loads.
  • Connect the circuit to loads.
    • Connect L of wattmeter-1 to A0.
    • Connect L of wattmeter-2 to B0.
    • Connect V of wattmeter-2 to C0.
  • Make connections in Delta configuration.
    • Connect A0 to B1.
    • Connect B0 to C1.
    • Connect C0 to A1.
  • Switch on the MCB and click on 'ADD' to add observations to the table.
